# Guest Experience Business

A working repository for two experience-design business concepts:

1. **Transat / Airline Guest Experience** — helping airlines create memorable low-cost moments in economy and premium economy.
2. **IRCC / New Canadian Welcome Experience** — redesigning the citizenship ceremony experience so it feels more like a national welcome than an administrative process.

## Core thesis

Organizations spend enormous effort making complex systems efficient. The next opportunity is to make those systems feel human.

For airlines, that means moving beyond price, schedule and operations to create moments passengers remember.

For citizenship ceremonies, that means separating the clerical process from the ceremonial experience so new Canadians and their families feel the emotional weight of the milestone.

## Signature lines

## Source of credibility

The concepts draw on Ron Shlien's experience as co-founder of Mad Science and Mad Science Productions, where live theatrical, educational and family experiences were designed for children, families, schools, theme parks, museums and major partners, including LEGO and NASA.

They also draw on Ron's experience presiding over Canadian citizenship ceremonies, where the challenge is not simply procedural delivery, but creating dignity, belonging, warmth and meaning at scale.

These are master working documents. They are designed to become pitch decks, executive summaries, proposal documents, pilot plans, scripts, and business-development outreach materials.
